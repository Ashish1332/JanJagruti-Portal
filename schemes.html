Schemes in Detail

Agriculture and Rural Development

Government schemes in the agricultural sector often focus on providing subsidies, and enhancing rural infrastucture to boost productivity and income.

Banking and Finance Sector

In the banking and finance sector, schemes aim to promote financial inclusion, offering services to the unbanked, providing credit facilities for small businesses.

Health Sector

Health schemes generally aim to improve healthcare accessibility, affordability, and quality, often including measures like the establishment of health centers.

Education Sector

Educational schemes typically target improvements in access, quality, and infrastructure, offering scholarships, upgrading school facilities.
